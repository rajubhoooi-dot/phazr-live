Sabse pehle main apne baare mein bata deti hu. Main 23 saal ki hu, aur mera figure 34-29-36 hai. Rang mera gora hai, aur jo bhi ladka ya mard mujhe ek baar dekh le, wo mera deewana ho jata hai. Lekin main itni aasani se kisi ke hath nahi aati. Main Punjab ke Ludhiana se hu. Is kahani mein main aapko batati hu, ki kaise mujhe papa ne checking ke bahane choda.
Baat kuch maheeno pehle ki hai. Mummy-papa, aur mera bhai kisi function mein gaye hue the. Mujhe office se ek important project mila tha, jis par abhi bahut kaam baaki tha. Isliye mere paas time nahi tha, to maine function par jaane se mana kar diya.
Ghar walo ke jaane ke baad maine kaam shuru kar diya, aur 3 ghante lagi rahi. Uske baad time dekha to 11 baj rahe the, aur mujhe neend aane lag gayi thi. Lekin kaam poora hone mein abhi bhi 2-3 ghante lagne waale the. Isliye maine socha naha kar fresh ho jati hu.
Fir main bathroom mein jaa kar naha kar aayi, aur uske baad red shorts aur white t-shirt pehan li. Ab main fir se kaam mein lag gayi. Shuru-shuru mein to main fresh feel kar rahi thi, lekin ek ghante baad neend fir se aane lagi.
Fir maine socha ki kyun na cigarette peete-peete kaam karu. Doston mujhe cigarette peete hue kabhi neend nahi aati. Lekin mere ghar pe is baat ka nahi pata tha ki main kabhi-kabhi cigarette peeti hu.
Fir maine apni chhupayi hui cigarette ki packet mein se ek cigarette nikali, aur usko jalaya. Ab main kash lagate hue kaam karne lagi. Meri concentration bhi badh gayi thi. Kaam karte-karte mujhe pata hi nahi chala ki kab papa ghar ke andar enter hue, aur kab mere room mein mere peeche aake khade ho gaye.
Darasal papa apna purse ghar par bhool gaye the, isliye wo mummy aur bhai ko function par chhodh kar wapas aaye the. Unke paas ghar ki chaabi rehti hai, isliye wo mujhe soyi hui samajh kar chup-chaap andar aaye the, taaki mai disturb na hou. Lekin yahan to unko kuch aur hi dekhne ko mil raha tha.
Fir unhone mujhe awaaz lagayi: Pritanka!
Unki awaaz sun kar meri gaand fatt gayi. Main peeche mudi, aur unko dekha. Mere muh mein cigarette thi, jo muh se gir gayi. Fir main boli-
Main: Papa aap yahan kaise? Aap to gaye the function par.
Papa: Mera purse reh gaya ghar par wo lene aaya tha. Lekin ye cigarette kab se pee rahi ho tum?
Main: Wo papa concentration nahi ban raha tha to friend ne kaha ki isse ban jayega.
Papa: Ye kya bakwas hai! Fir to saare hi peena shuru kar de. Sach batao kab se pee rahi ho.
Main: Papa aaj hi ek leke aayi hu friend se. Ek hi thi.
Papa: Jhooth mat bolo.
Maine cigarette ka packet apni shorts ki andar wali jeb mein rakha tha, jo baahar se nahi dikh raha tha. Mujhe laga yahan tak papa kabhi nahi pahunchenge. Lekin wo bole-
Papa: Idhar aao mujhe checking karni hai.
Ye sun kar main ghabra gayi ki kahin unke hath lag gaya packet to? But mujhe ye bhi tha, ki itni andar tak nahi jayenge. Fir papa chair par baith gaye, aur main unke paas jaake khadi ho gayi. Unhone mere ghutno par hath rakhe, aur dabate hue checking karne lage.
Pehle unhone mere ghutno par hath fera. Fir upar ki taraf aane lage. Unhone meri jaanghon par hath fera aur unko dabaya. Na-jaane kyun, lekin isse mujhe ajeeb si feeling aane lagi. Uske baad papa meri gaand par apne hath leke aaye, aur mere chootaon ko dabane lage. Isse main garam hone lagi.
Meri chut ne chudai ka swaad le rakha tha. Aur bahut waqt ho chuka tha chude hue, to ek mard ke sparsh se mujhe waisi wali feeling aane lagi.
Chootadon par hath ferte hue papa aage hath leke aaye, aur jaanghon par ferne lage. Wahin par cigarette ka packet tha. Jaise hi unko packet mehsoos hua wo bole-
Papa: Nikaal isko.
Main: Papa kuch nahi hai. Kapda hai wo.
Papa: Drama mat kar.
Main: Nahi papa, kuch nahi hai.
Papa: Ruk main apne aap nikaal deta hu.
Ye bol kar papa ne meri shorts ke andar apna hath daalne lage. Maine shorts ko kamar se kass kar pakad liya, taaki unka hath andar na jaye. Lekin papa bhi poori koshish karne lage hath andar daalne ki.
Jaise-taise papa ka hath shorts ke andar chala gaya. Maine socha peeche ho jati hu, to maine peeche ki taraf zor lagaya. Papa ne shorts kheencha, aur wo neeche utar gaya. Ab shorts meri gaand ke neeche aa chuka tha, aur meri white panty aaram se dikh rahi thi. Tabhi papa bole-
Papa: Mera hath packet tak pahunch chuka hai. Ab tu pakdi gayi hai. Zor lagane ka koi faida nahi hai. Zid chhodh de.
Ab mujhe bhi laga ki main pakdi to jaa hi chuki thi, to zor laga kar bhi koi faida nahi tha. Isliye main unke paas hoke seedhi khadi ho gayi. Meri shorts tight thi, to papa ne shorts thodi aur neeche ki, aur andar wali pocket mein hath daal kar cigarette ka packet baahar nikaal liya. Fir papa bole-
Papa: Tu to keh rahi thi ki tere paas ek hi cigarette hai. To fir ye koi baahar se aake daal gaya teri jeb mein.
Papa ne packet hath mein pakda tha. Tabhi unko packet ki ek side par kuch geela-geela mehsoos hua. Unhone ungli fer kar dekha, aur unko kuch chipchipa sa laga. Fir unhone meri shorts ki taraf dekha aur bole-
Papa: Ye geela kyun hai?
Maine bola: Pata nahi papa.
Aur ye bolte hue maine neeche dekha. Maine dekha ki meri panty chut wali jagah se geeli hui padi thi, kyunki papa ke chhoone se main uttejit ho gayi thi. Ye dekh kar maine papa ki taraf dekha, ki kahin unki nazar wahan na pad jaye.
