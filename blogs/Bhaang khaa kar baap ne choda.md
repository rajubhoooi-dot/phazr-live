Ye kahani kuch waqt pehle ki hai. Mere chacha foreign se aaye hue the. Wo kuch kaam ke silsile mein aaye the, isliye chachi aur bachho ko leke nahi aaye the. Unhe yahan par 3 din ka kaam tha, aur wo hamare ghar hi ruke hue the.
Pehli raat papa aur chacha sath baithe hue the. Wo hass-hass kar baatein kar rahe the. Chacha aur papa dono drink karte hai, lekin mummy ne unko pehle se bol diya tha ki drink nahi karni hai. Naa ghar par karni hai, aur naa hi baahar jaa kar. Baaton-baaton mein chacha ne papa se kaha-
Chacha: Bhai yaar maza nahi aa raha. Bolo na bhabhi se ki drink karne ki permission de de. Thoda suroor to chadhe.
Papa: Tu to jaanta hai, wo nahi maanengi.
Chacha: To fir kuch aur kar lete hai.
Papa: Kya karenge?
Chacha: Yahan se thodi door ek pakode ki dukan hai. Mast bhaang ke pakode banata hai wo. Agar tum kaho to thodi zyada bhaang waale pakode leke aau?
Papa: Haa, fir to maza aa jayega. Jaa le aa jaake.
Fir chacha gaye, aur aadhe ghante baad pakode leke wapas aaye. Mummy check karne aayi, aur pakode dekh kar wapas chali gayi. Wo nahi jaanti thi ki pakodo mein bhaang thi. Lekin main jaanti thi.
Fir dono maze se ek-ek karke saare pakode khaa gaye. Uske baad wo dono har ek baat par hasne lage. Unki bewajah hassi dekh kar main samajh gayi thi ki wo nashe mein the. Fir mera sone ka time ho gaya, aur main apne room mein jaake so gayi. Us waqt 10:30 baje the.
Takreeban 2 baje mere room mein kuch shor hua. Meri neend khuli to dekha papa mere room ke darwaze se andar aa rahe the. Wo ladkhada rahe the. Main unko dekh kar samajh gayi ki nashe ki wajah se wo galti se mere room ko apna samajh kar andar aa rahe the.
Darasal mera aur mummy-papa ka room dono bilkul sath lagte hai. To aksar hum ek-doosre ke room mein chale jaate hai. Isse pehle ki main papa ko batati ki ye mera room tha, wo mere sath aake bed par gir pade. Maine peeche ghoom kar unko awaaz di, lekin papa ulte ho kar besudh hoke lete rahe.
Mujhe laga wo nashe mein the, to so gaye honge. Isliye maine unko zyada nahi jagaya, aur khud bhi sone lagi. Kuch hi minute mein meri neend lag gayi. Kuch der baad meri neend fir se khuli. Mujhe apni jaanghon par kuch mehsoos ho raha tha. Maine dhyan se dekha, to papa meri jaanghon par hath fer rahe the.
Main left side muh karke leti hui hui thi, aur maine pajama aur t-shirt pehne hue the. Papa mere peeche chipak ke lete hue the, aur meri jaanghon par hath fira rahe the. Isse pehle main unko kuch kehti, wo bole-
Papa: Jaanu aaj bahut mann kar raha hai. Lelo na isko apni chut mein.
Mere papa meri mummy ko pyar se jaanu kehte hai. Khaas kar jab wo unko chodte hai, to jaanu-jaanu keh kar sab karte hai. Unke muh se jaanu shabd sun kar main samajh gayi ki wo mujhe mummy samajh rahe the. Aap soch rahe honge ki mujhe ye sab kaise pata. To janaab maine chhup kar kaafi baar mummy-papa ki chudai dekhi hui hai.
Ab mere paas do raaste the. Ya to main papa ko hosh mein laati, aur unko unke kamre mein jaane ke liye kehti. Ya fir unka mota lund jo mujhe meri gaand par mehsoos ho raha tha, usko apni chut mein leke maza karti.
Doston main koi doodh ki dhuli to hu nahi. Mujhe lund liye ko kaafi time ho gaya tha, aur main fingering karke guzara kar rahi thi. To maine socha kyun na lund ka maza le hi lu. Waise bhi papa hosh mein to the nahi, to unko kuch yaad to rehne wala nahi tha.
Aakhirkaar meri vasna ki aag rishton ke aage jeet gayi, aur maine papa se chudne ka fainsla kiya. Fir maine apna hath papa ke hath par rakha, aur unke hath ko kapdon ke upar se hi apni chut par rakh diya.
Mera aisa karna papa ke liye green signal tha. Pehle papa meri chut ko kapdon ke upar se ragadne lage. Isse main garam hone lagi, aur mere muh se siskiyan nikalne lagi. Fir papa ne mere pajame mein hath daal liya, aur panty mein hath daal kar meri chut ragadne lage. Isse to main pagal hone lagi.
Unke hatho ki movement ke hisab se main apni gaand aage-peeche karne lagi. Isse meri gaand unke khade lund par baar-baar dab jati. Thodi der baad papa ne mera pajama panty samet neeche kheenchna shuru kiya. Maine gaand utha kar unki help ki.
Ab main neeche se nangi thi. Papa bhi jaldi se neeche se nange ho gaye. Mujhe unka lund apne chootad par touch hota mehsoos ho raha tha. Fir papa ne meri ek taang uthayi, aur apni taang ke upar set kar li.
Fir unhone apna lund hath mein liya, aur meri chut par set kiya. Tabhi maine peeche dekha, to unki aankhen band thi. Matlab abhi bhi wo sab neend mein kar rahe the.
Fir papa ne zor ka dhakka maara, aur unka lund sarakta hua poora andar chala gaya. Meri zor ki cheekh nikal jati, agar main apne muh ko dabati nahi to. Agar cheekh nikalti, to papa ke jaagne ka khatra tha.
3-4 baar papa ne aise hi poora lund baahar nikaal kar andar dala. Mujhe dard ho raha tha, lekin main bas hmm hmm kar rahi thi. Fir papa ne ek hath mere chuche par dala, aur usko dabane lage. Neeche se unhone dhakke maarne shuru kar diye.
Kuch hi der mein unka lund chut mein adjust ho gaya, aur mujhe bahut maza aane laga. Wo chodte gaye, aur main ahh ahh karne maze leti rahi. Main bhi gaand rhythm mein peeche kar rahi thi, taaki lund poora andar jaye.
Kamre mein thap-thap aur ahh ahh ki awaaze aa rahi thi. 20 minute papa ne mujhe taang utha kar choda. Is dauran main 2 baar jhad gayi. Fir mujhe apni chut mein papa ka garam-garam laava mehsoos hua. Unhone poora maal meri chut mein khaali kar diya, aur muh doosri taraf karke so gaye.
